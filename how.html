How Lockrion Works

Lockrion allows issuers to create transparent, time-locked reward programs on Solana. Everything is fixed in advance. Nothing can be changed after launch. The rules are executed automatically by smart contracts.

The Reward Is Fixed

When an issuance is created, the total reward amount is defined in advance. This amount never changes.

If the reserve is declared as 100,000 USDC, it will always remain exactly 100,000 USDC. It cannot be increased or reduced after deployment.

The Reward Must Be Fully Funded

Before anyone can participate, the full reward must already be locked on-chain.

If the reserve is not fully funded, participation is not possible. There is no partial funding and no hidden adjustments.

Participation Is Time-Locked

Each issuance has a clearly defined start date and maturity date.

You can deposit only during that window. Deposits after maturity are rejected. Withdrawals before maturity are not allowed.

Distribution Is Automatic

Rewards are calculated automatically by the smart contract. There are no manual decisions, no changes, and no governance votes.

The system follows predefined rules and executes them exactly as written.

After Maturity

Once the maturity date is reached, participants can claim their rewards. Deposits can be withdrawn according to the rules.

Unclaimed rewards follow predefined logic. Nothing can be altered after deployment.

No Hidden Control

After an issuance is deployed, no one can change its parameters. There are no administrative keys and no emergency overrides.

The contract executes exactly as defined. The outcome depends only on the declared rules and on-chain state.
